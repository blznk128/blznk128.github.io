About me

My name is Benito Borunda Jr, but everyone calls me Benny. I was born in Perryton, Texas, and lived there for 14 years until I moved to Utah with my family. I currently live in Taylorsville, Utah. I've been with my awesome wife for over 10 years, and we have two beautiful girls. I'm currently a beverage delivery driver. I've been in the beverage industry for almost 10 years. This job has taught me to be a hard worker. You have to work in the extreme heat and cold, rain and snow and everything in between until the job is done. It also requires about 10 or more hours a day, sometimes that shoots up to 65 or more hours a week. Lets just say I rather have a career using my brain instead of my back. I'm currently enrolled in the University of Utah web development bootcamp. I'm amazed by how much I love coding and building websites. I'm looking forward to a new career in web development. I'm also looking forward to working with others. I'm an easy going guy and enjoy communicating with people.
